Tata Consultancy Services provides consulting, IT services, enterprise applications, cloud, cybersecurity, analytics, engineering, and business process services for clients across banking, retail, manufacturing, communications, life sciences, and public services.

Revenue depends on client technology budgets, large deal wins, renewal rates, offshore delivery execution, talent availability, utilization, pricing, and foreign exchange rates. Slower discretionary spending can delay transformation programs.

TCS benefits from global delivery scale, deep client relationships, process maturity, and a strong balance sheet. The company emphasizes training, automation, and domain expertise to protect delivery quality and margins.

Key risks include client concentration, wage inflation, visa restrictions, cybersecurity obligations, currency volatility, intense competition, and rapid technology change including generative AI-driven service model disruption.
